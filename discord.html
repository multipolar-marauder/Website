We have a discord server! If you like to just casually discuss political issues or meet like minded people who are interested in politics, please join our Discord chat! We may also host events etc. on there as well!

Rules

  2. Be respectful of others
  3. Be tolerant of other people's beliefs
  4. When having serious discussions, be civil
  5. No NSFW or obscene content
  6. Have fun with the nicknames! For identification purposes, use brackets denoting your full name, if you are comfortable with using your name
  7. Introduce yourself in the introductions channel!
