MultiPolar Marauder is a McMaster club dedicated towards promoting dialogue towards geo poltics, world affairs and other political issues that concern humanity. Oftentimes we have a lot of tension between opposing sides who tend to mis-interpret each other's views without listening. Our goal at Mulipolar Marauder is to facilitate meaningful content and promote intelligent discussions. The mission here is to take various viewpoints and analyze them to come to a better informed conclusion which will ultimately make you a more politically informed person.
